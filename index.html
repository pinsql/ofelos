Welcome to Ofelos

Founded in 2021 by Dr. Gerassimos Fourlanos, Ofelos Ltd is a story of personal struggle turned into a commercial venture. Having suffered from diabetes since 1990, and struggling to find treats that are diabetic-friendly, the founder embarked on a journey to serve this need.

After 10 years of vigorous and comprehensive research, Ofelos LTD was birthed. Ofelos LTD is a company dedicated to providing pure, keto & diabetic-friendly treats to people suffering from diabetes and their friends and family.

Our Bakery is located at Highway Mall, along Uhuru Highway in Nairobi. Our products are stocked in several Healthy U stores and retailers around the country as well as in Aratus Health Limited (DiaBeters), Goodlife Pharmacy, Onn The Way, Quickmart, Art Caffe Market and many more.

The main ingredients in Ofelos cookies are low glycemic index and glycemic load.
